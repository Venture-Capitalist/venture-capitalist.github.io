Join ShadowCrystal

No experience needed. No cost to join. Just curiosity, commitment, and a drive to build something real.

Are You Eligible?

🎓

Grades 9–12

Open to all students from 9th through 12th grade — freshmen and seniors alike are welcome from day one.

🏫

Any School

Urbana, Damascus, Clarksburg, Richard Montgomery, or any other school in the area — your campus doesn't matter.

🤖

No Experience Necessary

Students who are driven to learn and succeed will always thrive here, even over those with prior experience.

Your Path to Joining

1

Submit Your Interest

Fill out the interest form below with your name, grade, school, and which divisions interest you most. This lets us match you with the right team and keep you updated on upcoming events.

2

Attend an Info Session or Camp

We'll reach out to invite you to an intro session where you'll meet the team, hear about projects, and get a feel for how ShadowCrystal operates before committing to anything.

3

Choose Your Division

After meeting the team, you'll choose a primary division — Terrestrial, Aerospace, Marine, or Business — based on your interests. You can always explore other divisions as you grow within the organization.

4

Start Building

Jump in. Experienced team members, both adult and student, will guide you on your first projects, and you'll have immediate access to our parts warehouse, tools, and a team of students who want to see you succeed.

We'll follow up within a few days. Questions? Contact us directly.
