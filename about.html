About ShadowCrystal

A student-led nonprofit with the job of giving every young engineer deserves a place to build, fail, and grow, regardless of what school they attend or how much money they can spend. For an in-depth description of our organization, please refer to our 2026 Q2 Business Plan for more details.

🗻

Mission

Enable high school students to dive deeper into STEM through confidence built by competence and networking with experienced mentors and like-minded peers.

🔭

Vision

Develop into an empowering community hub that kickstarts and reinforces student passion and education within engineering and business fields.

🎯

Purpose

Students in Montgomery and Frederick Counties lack access to mentorship and resources for hands-on engineering. ShadowCrystal exists because we refuse to let that continue.

Founding Team

Founder
Trystan Lord

Former Team Lead of FRC Team 8726, responsible for robot programming and electrical wiring. Trystan founded ShadowCrystal to expand the access to engineering education that transformed his own high school experience.

Co-Founder
Tikhon Kozlov

President of the Urbana High School Rocketry Club with three years of model rocketry experience. Tikhon leads ShadowCrystal's Aerospace Rocketry Subdivision and the Marine Division.

Co-Founder
Victor Wang

Award-winning drone builder whose self-built drones made it to ISEF and the IEEE SERA 2026 Conference. Victor brings deep hands-on expertise to ShadowCrystal's Aerospace Drone Subdivision.

Where We Operate

ShadowCrystal serves students across Montgomery County and Frederick County, Maryland, an area with demonstrated student interest in STEM and a clear lack of community-accessible engineering programs.

📍

Montgomery County

Home to high-performing school clusters whose students have excelled in state STEM competitions.

📍

Frederick County

A critical region serving Maryland's biotechnology, aerospace, and defense industries, with budding potential in STEM competition leagues like FRC.
